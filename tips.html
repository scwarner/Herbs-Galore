Growing Tips

  • Choose herbs you’ll use. There’s no point in planting cilantro if you are not going to use it, no matter how easy it may be to grow.
  • Full sun or partial shade? Know how much sunlight your designated herb garden will get and buy plants that are suited to that environment.
  • Pick the right pot. If you are planting in containers, choose a pot that will be large enough for your growing plant, otherwise you may have to repot midseason.
  • Keeping pinching. Unless you are growing herbs solely for aesthetics, don’t let your plants flower. Herbs aren’t as tasty once they’ve gone to seed. Pinch back plants to promote bushiness.
